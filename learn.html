Learn why we chose lobsters!

Why lobsters you may be asking yourself? First off, we are based out of Portland, Maine. If you don't know much about Maine then you may not know that it is pretty much the lobster capital of the USA. Our promise to you is to provide you with fresh lobster that we pull right out of our backyard, the ocean. We believe that if you aren't capable of making the trip to us to enjoy the freshest lobster around that we will deliver it to you. Fresh from the ocean right to your table!
